Having Fun & Staying Fit

We recognize the importance of exercise, especially for a busy work environment like ours. That's why we're taking large strides to keep our team happy and healthy. To promote physical and mental well being, BPG has started its own flag football team. Every Tuesday, we hit the field to take on teams from all over the valley. Office bragging rights are on the line! In addition to events encouraging fitness, we're proud to now offer discounts for gym memberships to our employees who want to stay healthy.
